# Procurement & Supply Chain Performance Analysis (Excel Dashboard)

A professional procurement portfolio project showcasing **data-driven analysis** of procurement and supply chain operations using Microsoft Excel, Power Query, and Pivot Tables.

This project evaluates key performance areas including **cost efficiency, supplier performance, delivery reliability, product quality, and compliance**, delivering actionable insights to improve overall supply chain effectiveness.

Two interactive Excel dashboards were developed to visualize procurement overview and detailed supplier performance metrics.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze procurement spend and identify cost savings opportunities
- Evaluate supplier performance across delivery, quality, and compliance
- Measure operational efficiency using critical supply chain KPIs
- Identify risks and inefficiencies in the procurement process
- Provide data-driven recommendations for process improvement

---

## Key KPIs Analyzed

| KPI                          | Description |
|-----------------------------|-----------|
| **Total Spend**             | Overall procurement expenditure |
| **Total Savings & Savings %** | Cost savings achieved through negotiation |
| **On-Time Delivery %**      | Percentage of orders delivered on schedule |
| **Average Lead Time (Days)**| Average time from order to delivery |
| **Defect Rate**             | Percentage of defective or rejected items |
| **Compliance Rate**         | Adherence to procurement policies and standards |
| **Total Orders by Status**  | Breakdown of order statuses (Completed, Pending, Cancelled, etc.) |

## Key Insights

1. **Supplier Performance Challenges**  
   The analysis revealed an **On-Time Delivery rate of approximately 61%**, meaning nearly 40% of orders experience delays. Several suppliers also showed consistently high defect rates, leading to increased costs, rework, and supply chain disruptions.

2. **Cost Efficiency is Stable but Not Optimized**  
   Average savings rate stands at **~8%**, which has remained relatively flat over time. High spend concentration in categories such as **MRO and Office Supplies** indicates strong potential for further cost optimization through better sourcing strategies.

---

## Recommendations

1. **Implement Supplier Performance Management**  
   Introduce a formal **supplier scorecard** to track delivery, quality, and compliance. Establish clear Service Level Agreements (SLAs) with performance targets and penalties. Prioritize high-performing suppliers and reduce dependency on underperformers.

2. **Strengthen Strategic Sourcing**  
   Adopt supplier consolidation, bulk purchasing, and long-term contracts for high-spend categories to improve negotiation leverage and drive sustainable cost savings.

3. **Improve Planning and Quality Control**  
   Enhance demand forecasting to reduce order cancellations and pending orders. Implement stricter quality inspection processes and standardized acceptance criteria to lower defect rates and boost supply chain reliability.

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates the power of **data-driven procurement analysis** in uncovering hidden inefficiencies and driving meaningful improvements in supply chain performance.

By focusing on supplier management, strategic sourcing, and operational planning, organizations can significantly reduce costs, improve delivery reliability, and build a more resilient supply chain.
